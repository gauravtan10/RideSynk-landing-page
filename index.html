The Idea

Your riding group, always in sync.

RideSynk is made for bikers who ride together. No more losing each other at traffic lights or wrong turns.

See where every rider is, follow the same route, and stay together — all without touching your phone while riding.

Real-Time
Rider Tracking

Instantly see the exact location of everyone in your pack. Whether you're miles ahead or catching up, you're always connected.

Group
Navigation

Share a single route. When the leader takes a detour or stops, the entire group's map updates seamlessly. No confusion, just riding.

Safety-Focused
Riding

Get instant alerts if a rider stops suddenly or needs help. Your group stays aware.

Auto Reroute
Smart Routing

If you hit a red light while the pack goes green, RideSynk calculates the exact path to catch up safely.

Why RideSynk?

Because group rides should be fun — not stressful.

RideSynk was born from a simple problem: losing riders in a group, the danger of checking phones while riding, and no app made just for bikers. We're building the tool every rider needs — easy to use, built for the road, made for riding together.
