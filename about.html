About RideSynk

RideSynk was created with one simple idea: make group riding safer and more connected.

When people ride together — whether on motorcycles, bicycles, or road trips — staying connected can be challenging. Riders may get separated, lose track of each other, or struggle with navigation.

RideSynk solves this problem by allowing riding groups to:

  • Share their live location in real time
  • Track each other during rides
  • Navigate routes together
  • Stay connected and ride safely

Our goal is to make group riding simpler, safer, and more enjoyable.

RideSynk is currently an independent project developed and operated by Gaurav Tanwar.

We are continuously improving the platform and building new features to support the riding community.

Thank you for being part of the RideSynk journey.
