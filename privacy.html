Privacy Policy for RideSynk

Last Updated: March 16, 2026

RideSynk ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how RideSynk collects, uses, and protects your data when you use our mobile application and related services.

By using RideSynk, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

To provide our services, we may collect the following types of information:

Personal Information

When you create an account or use RideSynk, we may collect:

  • Name or username
  • Email address
  • Phone number

Location Information

RideSynk is designed to help riding groups stay connected and track each other in real time. Therefore we may collect:

  • Live GPS location
  • Route and trip history
  • Navigation and ride path data

Location data is used only for providing ride tracking and navigation features.

Device Information

We may automatically collect certain device information including:

  • IP address
  • Device type
  • Operating system
  • App usage statistics
  • Crash logs

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage your RideSynk account
  • Enable real-time location sharing between riding group members
  • Provide navigation and route tracking features
  • Improve app performance and reliability
  • Ensure security and prevent misuse
  • Communicate important updates about the service

3. Location Sharing

RideSynk allows users to share their live location with members of their riding group. Location sharing only occurs when:

  • You join a ride group
  • You enable location services
  • You choose to share your location with other members

You can stop sharing your location by leaving the ride group or disabling location access.

4. Third-Party Services

RideSynk uses trusted third-party services to operate the platform, including:

  • Mapbox – for maps, navigation, and geocoding
  • Supabase – for secure database and backend infrastructure
  • AI services for ride analysis and system improvements (if applicable)

These services may process limited data necessary for their functionality.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your personal information using modern security practices. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

6. Data Retention

We retain user data only as long as necessary to provide the service and improve the RideSynk platform. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

7. Children's Privacy

RideSynk is intended for users 18 years or older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under the age of 18.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing

Requests can be made by contacting us.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted within the application or on the website.
